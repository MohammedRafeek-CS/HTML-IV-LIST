TECHNO PARK INDUSTRIAL VISIT

Infopark is a well-known and innovative technology park located in the southern Indian state of Kerala, specifically in the city of Kochi. Established in 2004, it has grown to become a prominent hub for information technology and related industries in the region. This sprawling IT park spans over 260 acres and is designed to foster technological growth and innovation. Infopark has played a pivotal role in transforming Kochi into a major IT destination, attracting both domestic and international companies. The park provides a dynamic environment for businesses, fostering collaboration and innovation among its tenants. Its infrastructure includes state-of-the-art office spaces, IT-enabled services, and top-notch amenities.
